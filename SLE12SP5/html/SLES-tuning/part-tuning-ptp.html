19 Precision Time Protocol

For network environments, it is vital to keep the computer and other devices' clocks synchronized and accurate. There are several solutions to achieve this, for example the widely used Network Time Protocol (NTP) described in Book “Administration Guide”, Chapter 26 “Time Synchronization with NTP”.
